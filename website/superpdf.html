Frequently Asked Questions

Is Super PDF Tools free to use?

Yes, all our basic PDF tools are completely free to use with no hidden charges. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will remain free.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All files are processed securely and automatically deleted from our servers after processing. We don't store or share your files.

What file sizes can I process?

You can process files up to 50MB with our free tools. For larger files, consider splitting them into smaller parts or check our premium options.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required! All our tools work directly in your web browser on any device - computer, tablet, or smartphone.
